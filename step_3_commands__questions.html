A Day in the Classroom

Good morning, students! Today is an exciting day in our English class. Let's start with a cheerful "Hello" and a big smile. Now, it's time to stand up and give your classmates a high five. Great! Now, hands up if you're ready for an adventure!

Before we begin, let's clap our hands together three times. Fantastic! Now, turn around and face the front. It's time to practice some English commands.

First, the teacher says, "Everybody, stamp your feet!" That's it! Feel the energy. Now, let's stretch out your arms like superheroes. Awesome!

Okay, now it's time to focus. Everyone, sit down quietly and open your English books. Today, we're going to read a fun story and learn some new words. Can you find the page? Yes, that's it!

As we read, the teacher might say, "Please, repeat after me." Great job! Remember to be quiet when someone is reading or speaking.

Now, let's try something fun. Stand up and sing a song together. Feel the rhythm! After that, we'll practice writing some words on the board. Get your pencils ready!

Oh no, the bell is ringing. It's time to go out and enjoy the rest of the day. But before you leave, say "Goodbye" to your friends.
